Hi, I’m Sophia Suzuki-Jones

But my students call me Ms. Jay. I’m a wanderer, forever learning, avocado eating teacher living in foggy San Francisco.

I was lucky enough to be born and raised in Honolulu, Hawaii! While growing up on the beautiful island of Oahu, I developed a love for the ocean and a deep passion for environmental conservation. And so, after graduating high school from Punahou School, I left Hawaii to attend the University of Redlands, where I received a B.S. in Environmental Science and minors in both Government and Spatial Studies- Geographic Information Systems.
